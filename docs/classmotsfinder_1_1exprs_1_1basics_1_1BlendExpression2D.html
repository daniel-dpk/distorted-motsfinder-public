Detailed Description

Blend between two expressions based on a third.

Represents an expression of the form

\[ f(\mathbf{x}) = f_1(\mathbf{x}) (1 - \beta(\mathbf{x})) + \beta(\mathbf{x}) f_2(\mathbf{x}). \]
