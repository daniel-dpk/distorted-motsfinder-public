Detailed Description

Brill-Lindquist 3-metric of two non-rotating, non-spinning black holes.

This metric due to Brill and Lindquist [1] describes, as implemented here, two non-spinning black holes at a moment of time-symmetry before evolving to a head-on collision.

The ADM mass of the metric (specifically, the ADM mass of the asymptotic end ) will be m1+m2.

Parameters
d(float) Distance parameter. This represents the coordinate distance of the two punctures. If it is chosen low enough, a common horizon forms which is usually interpreted as the two black holes having merged into a single black hole. Separate inner MOTSs may still exist. The value at which a common horizon forms for equal mass black holes has been determined to low accuracy in [1] and with higher accuracy in [2], see Table 2.2 therein. It reads approximately .
m1(float) Mass parameter of first black hole. Default is 1.
m2(float) Mass parameter of second black hole. Default is 1.
axis({'x', 'y', 'z'}, optional) Symmetry axis on which to place the two punctures. Default is 'z'.
