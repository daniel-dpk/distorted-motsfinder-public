Detailed Description

Extrinsic curvature of the Kerr slice in Kerr-Schild form.

This is eq. (3) of [2], with difference in overall sign convention taken into account here, obtained by comparison of a = 0 case with Schwarschild.

References

[2] Moreno, Claudia, Dar\'{i}o N\'{u}{n}ez, and Olivier Sarbach. "Kerr- Schild type initial data for black holes with angular momenta". Class. Quant. Grav. 19 (2002) 6059-6073.
